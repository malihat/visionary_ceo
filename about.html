About Me

Lance is known for provoking others to challenge themselves to reach for their goals with intention. Lance coaches that a simple culmination of focus, hard work and explicit dedication can start by simply taking action. He commonly asks his team and coaching students “what are you doing”, Which some may see as a rhetorical question and quite random.

Lance sees it as a way of provoking someone to constantly evaluate their state of mind. In his coaching Lance frequently refers to his core belief that a constant state of self awareness is paramount in the quest to remain focused. This constant reinforcement has been a catalyst and stimulant in his personal road to success.

Overcoming all obstacles that cross his path, Lance has developed an unmatched insight on connecting, addressing, and conquering common setbacks and personal struggles of aspiring real estate investors. A man keen on learning every aspect of an ever changing industry to deliver YOU a key to unlock mental barriers, a defined strategy to real estate investment success or even the door to your first home. To be a man with a vision is a job within itself. Lance learned early what his purpose is and since that realization he lives everyday never fearful of defeat and the constant push to make his vision a reality. Deservedly coining the title of the Visionary CEO.
